A simple guide to protecting your online privacy and keeping your personal information safe.

Email | Passwords | 2FA | Search Engine | Social Media | Text Messaging

This site is here to help you take small, easy steps to safeguard your digital life. We’ve tested what works best and broken it down into straightforward tips anyone can follow. Whether you’re comfortable with technology or just getting started, we’ve designed this site to be helpful for everyone.

To make things easier, we’ve created two versions of every guide: detailed steps for those who want to dive deeper into the "how" and "why" behind each tip (geek), and quick tips for those who just want a simple solution they can use right away (yogi).

Email

Move to a more secure email provider.

Passwords

Invest in a password manager.

2FA

Turn on two-factor authentication.
