Social Media

Why Bluesky | How to Make the Switch | Best Practices | Additional Articles

Why Leave X and Facebook?

Social media platforms like X (formerly Twitter) and Facebook have become synonymous with invasive data collection. These platforms track your every move, from the posts you like to the links you click, building detailed profiles that are sold to advertisers. Worse yet, they’ve been at the center of numerous scandals involving data breaches and misuse of personal information.

For years, these platforms have prioritized profit over privacy. Facebook’s role in the Cambridge Analytica scandal and X’s recent policy changes allowing for increased data sharing are just the tip of the iceberg. If you’re serious about protecting your digital footprint, it’s time to consider alternatives.

Why Bluesky?

Bluesky is a decentralized social media platform built with user privacy in mind. Unlike traditional platforms, Bluesky doesn’t rely on invasive tracking or targeted advertising. Instead, it uses cutting-edge technology to give users control over their data while fostering a healthier online community.

  • Decentralized by Design: Bluesky operates on the AT Protocol (Authenticated Transfer Protocol), which allows users to own their data and move between platforms seamlessly.

  • No Ads or Tracking: Bluesky doesn’t track your activity or sell your data to advertisers. Your interactions remain private.

  • Upcoming Flashes Feature: Soon, Bluesky will introduce Flashes—a short-form video feature designed to rival Instagram Reels and TikTok. This will allow you to share creative content without compromising your privacy.

  • A Healthier Community: Bluesky’s moderation tools empower users to curate their own experience, reducing harassment and misinformation.

How to Make the Switch

  1. Create an account on Bluesky by visiting blueskyweb.xyz. You may need an invite code, which can often be found through friends or online communities.
  2. Set up your profile and start following accounts that interest you. Many creators and organizations are already migrating to Bluesky!
  3. If you’re leaving X or Facebook, let your followers know where they can find you on Bluesky before deactivating your old accounts.
  4. Explore upcoming features like Flashes for short-form video content—perfect for replacing Instagram Reels or TikTok.

Best Practices for Social Media Privacy

  • Avoid sharing sensitive personal information like your location or financial details on any platform.
  • Use strong, unique passwords for each social media account and enable two-factor authentication (2FA).
  • Regularly review your privacy settings to ensure you’re sharing only what you intend to.
  • If possible, use a VPN when accessing social media to mask your IP address.
  • Encourage friends and family to join privacy-focused platforms like Bluesky for a safer online experience.
