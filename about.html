About

This website was created as a way to help my friends become more digitally secure. It chronicles the steps I've taken to lock down my family's personal data and who has access to it. I specify the tools, applications, and services that I've determined work best for us. However, there will never be a one size fits all approach. You have to do your own risk assessment and determine where your comfort level lies. There must always be a balance between security and convenience.
